The discipline

Why rules beat reactions

01

Every signal traces to a rule

No vague judgement calls. A name only enters Buy, Sell/Trim, Insight or Movers because it crossed a threshold defined in advance, never because of a hunch after the fact.

02

Sourced, or it does not get published

Every fact is dated and footnoted: Yahoo Finance for prices and fundamentals, SEC 13F filings via Dataroma for what the world's tracked value investors are actually doing.

03

Written for the century, not the quarter

The Field Note asks one question about every business it profiles: will this still matter in a hundred years? That lens filters out most of the noise before it reaches you.
